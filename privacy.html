Privacy Policy

Preface

This privacy policy is in place to inform you of the privacy practices that I, Faunter, employ when operating and maintaining the website https://www.crowncrates.com.

Cookies and information collection

This website uses Google Analytics. Google Analytics helps me measure how you interact with the website. I use this service to improve and refine the site's content and functionality. As you navigate the website, Google Analytics provides me with means to record information about your visit. This information includes, but is not limited to: the pages you've seen, the date and time of your visit, and how long you stuck around for. Google Analytics uses Cookies to remember what you've done on, and how you've interacted with, the website. However, this information is anonymous, and neither I nor Google uses it to personally identify you. For further details, please refer to Google's partner sites policy.

Data use and sharing

The information generated by these Cookies is sent to Google, which they then store on their servers. Google uses this information to evaluate your use of the website, compile reports on website activity, and provide me with other related services. Google may also transfer this information to third parties where required to do so by law, or where such third parties process the information on Google's behalf. Again, Google does not associate your IP address with any other data held by Google. You can learn more about Google's privacy policy over at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Advertising

At this time, the website does not make use of advertisements in any capacity. Therefore, I do not use the information collected by Google to identify you in any way, or to serve you any targeted ads.

Closing

I reserve the right to change, edit, modify, or update the contents of this privacy policy at any time. If you have any questions or concerns, please feel free to contact me.
