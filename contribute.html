Contribute

The purpose of this website is to make the crown crate item drop rates publicly available for free and without advertisements.

Crown crates are an extremely niche topic, and the audience interested in them is incredibly limited. I spend a lot of time and effort in order to maintain the drop rates and provide them to you. If you like what I'm doing here and want to contribute, your help would mean a great deal to me and I would appreciate it a lot. 100% of your support will go towards future web-related expenses, as well as further upkeep of the addon and the item drop rates website.

Thank you.
